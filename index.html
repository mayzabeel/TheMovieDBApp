About

An app for cinephiles and movie hobbyists. This version of the app is very simple and limited to show the list of upcoming movies. The app is feeding with content from The Movie Database (TMDb) .

The Movie Database (TMDb) is a community built movie and TV database. Every piece of data has been added by our amazing community dating back to 2008.
